About Us

"BIHAR RAJ" is a digital media platform . This is an initiative taken by youth of Bihar. It's aim is to give voice to the grassroots issues of Bihar, which have gone away from the mainstream in the corporate world

It is our commitment to be completely impartial. Also the common man should become its cornerstone.And through ideological empowerment, social revolution can be started till the last ends of the society.
